SWASTHYA


OUR MISSION


We are here to care for you . Putting More Care Into Your Health.

“Prepare and prevent, don’t repair and repent.”
The aim of our Project Swasthya is take care of you . As we all know when any accident occur , in emergency situations the hospital has collect necessary details about Patient . A lot of time of goes into that . So we have an idea , to save that time . We will provide an E-card to our user to our user which he/she can carry along with them and in emergency situation the hospital staff can you that to know the details about the patient .




OUR VISION

“Vision without action is merely a dream.”
Our vision is to reach out in all the corners of our country and provide a unique "Health-Card" to every citizen, like any other goverment documents. It will not only help them in having an organised documentations of their medical history but it can also save lives by reducing paper works in critical situations such as in case of some accidents or mishap. We are looking to make a card for all the medical needs and records of our clients. A card that every one can afford and use.
